Once upon a time in a land of Ballz there was a great warrior by the name of HercuBall. 
           For many years they lived in peace, until the Fire Nation attacked.
Now after taking a long break HercuBall has decided he's fed up with the abysmal rule.
    He is now on a quest to free the land of Ballz from cruelty and save the world.
           But his first trial begins when the skeleton guard captain appears!
